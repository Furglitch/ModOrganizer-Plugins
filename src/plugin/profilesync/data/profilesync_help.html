The last change made is not synchronising to other profiles.

Profile Sync runs on a few specific events, when a mod is installed, removed, enabled/disabled or reordered through drag and drop. If another action causes a change, it may not trigger a sync. To ensure one is triggered, siwtching the enabled state of a mod back and forth once should solve the issue.
